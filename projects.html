Public Editor

"Public Editor is an online editing and review system allowing the public to openly assess the credibility of news articles and organizations, and share their results with the world." My role for Public Editor was in the Visualization & Data Analysis sub-team. We performed EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis) in order to help create a front-end visualization for end-users. I helped the team clean and organize the data, as well as work on an interactive button using HTML/CSS/Javascript.
