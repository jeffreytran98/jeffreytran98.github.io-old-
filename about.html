About Me - My Passions and Personality

I am a senior at the University of California, Berkeley studying Cognitive Science and Data Science. In the Summer and Spring of 2020, I was introduced to both the worlds of data and development. In the Fall of 2020, I’ve challenged myself with the task of becoming competent with the skills of a Data Analyst as well as a Web Developer.

Outside of academia, I’d describe myself as an athlete, musician, and video-game enthusiast! I’m the captain of Berkeley’s Division 1 esports Valorant team, a member of Cal Band, and have competed and participated in numerous athletic events for all my life from martial arts to swimming to Spartan Races!
